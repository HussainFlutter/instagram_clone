# Instagram Clone

A feature-rich Instagram clone built with Flutter and Firebase, replicating core functionalities of the popular social media platform. This app allows users to create, edit, delete posts, manage profiles, follow others, search for users by their username, and interact with comments and replies.

## Features

### **User Features**
- **Post Creation**:
  - Upload and share images as posts, including captions.
  - Edit and delete your posts as needed.

- **Profile Management**:
  - Edit your profile, including updating your profile picture and bio.
  - View your own posts, followers, and following.

- **Follow System**:
  - Follow other users and view their posts in your feed.
  - See who is following you and who you're following.

- **Search**:
  - Search for users by their username to follow or view their profile.

- **Comment and Reply**:
  - Create, update, and delete comments on posts.
  - Reply to comments for deeper interaction.

- **User Feed**:
  - View posts from people you follow in your personalized feed.

- **Likes**:
  - Like and unlike posts.

---

## Technologies Used
- **Flutter**: Framework for cross-platform app development.
- **Dart**: Programming language for Flutter apps.
- **Firebase Authentication**: For user login and registration.
- **Firebase Firestore**: To store user posts, comments, followers, and other app data.
- **Firebase Storage**: To store images and media files uploaded by users.

---

## Known Limitations
While the app is highly functional, there are a few small imperfections:
- **Network Issues**: Slow or interrupted internet connections might cause delays in post updates or media uploads.
